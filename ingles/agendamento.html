SCHEDULE

Scheduling is the procedure adopted by the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN) to organize and facilitate student participation in institutional activities, events, and actions. Through this process, the institution seeks to ensure the proper use of available spaces, resources, and schedules, guaranteeing efficiency, planning, and equity in meeting demands.

Scheduling must be carried out according to the guidelines established by the institution, respecting deadlines, criteria, and availability previously defined. This practice contributes to the smooth functioning of academic and cultural activities, as well as promoting organization and transparency in institutional processes.

Thus, scheduling is an essential tool for managing IFRN activities, promoting the orderly participation of the academic community and compliance with institutional norms.
