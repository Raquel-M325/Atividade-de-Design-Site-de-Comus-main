EVENTS

The events held by the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN) constitute spaces for integration, learning, and appreciation of students' talents. These activities aim to promote the exchange of experiences, encourage culture, and foster the active participation of the academic community in actions that contribute to the comprehensive education of students.

Aimed at students in integrated high school, subsequent high school, and undergraduate programs, the institutional events are organized in a way that guarantees inclusion and diversity, without age restrictions, allowing everyone to express their skills, creativity, and musicality. These moments strengthen the bond between students and the institution, as well as stimulate a sense of belonging and cooperation.

Thus, IFRN events reaffirm the institution's commitment to promoting culture, education, and citizenship, offering opportunities for students to actively participate in academic and institutional life.
