SELECTION PROCESSES

The selection processes promoted by the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN) aim to ensure democratic, transparent, and equitable access to the opportunities offered by the institution. These selections are intended for students regularly enrolled in integrated secondary education, subsequent secondary education, and undergraduate courses, without age restrictions, respecting the principles of inclusion, equal opportunities, and broad participation of the academic community.

Through the selection processes, IFRN seeks to identify and select candidates interested in participating in institutional projects, activities, and actions, ensuring that participation occurs in an organized manner and according to the criteria established in the call for applications. The stages of the process are conducted with clarity and impartiality, aiming to provide students with a formative experience aligned with the institution's educational values.
